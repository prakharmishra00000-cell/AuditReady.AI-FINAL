AI Audit Risk Scan

ALL PLANS

Upload documents or connect cloud storage → AI scans against your framework → instant risk dashboard with gap details and recommended actions.

  1. Select your compliance framework (SOC 2, GDPR, ISO 27001, HIPAA, PCI DSS, NIST)
  2. Connect Google Drive / AWS S3 / OneDrive / Notion or upload files directly
  3. Click "Start Autonomous Compliance Scan"
  4. Watch the live terminal log as AI scans each document
  5. Review the results dashboard – score, gaps found, risk levels
  6. Click "Export Auditor Evidence Package" to download the report

Active Remediation (AI Fix)

ALL PLANS

Don't just see the gap – fix it instantly. AI drafts the exact legal clause, config change, or policy amendment needed to resolve each compliance failure.

  1. After a scan, find any failed document in the results table
  2. Click "Fix Now" on the failed row
  3. Review the AI-generated amendment clause in the modal
  4. Choose: Copy Clause / Download File Fix (.patch / .tf) / Send via DocuSign (Scale+)
  5. Document status updates to ✅ Passed in the dashboard automatically

Send via DocuSign

SCALE & ENTERPRISE

After the AI drafts a contract amendment, send it directly to the vendor for e-signature – all from within the platform. No switching to email.

  1. Open a failed document's remediation modal via "Fix Now"
  2. Review the AI-drafted amendment clause
  3. Click the blue "Send via DocuSign" button
  4. System creates a DocuSign envelope and notifies the vendor via email
  5. Vendor signs digitally – the contract is updated and legally binding

Live Auditor Mode

SCALE & ENTERPRISE

Share a cryptographically signed, read-only portal with Deloitte, EY, or your CPA. They see your full evidence package – no email chains, no PDF bundles.

  1. Go to Auditor Mode in the left sidebar
  2. Enter the auditor's firm name, email, and access scope
  3. Set portal expiry (7 / 14 / 30 days)
  4. Click "Generate Secure Portal Link"
  5. Copy the cryptographic URL – share with your auditor
  6. Auditor sees evidence with SHA-256 hashes, read-only, no downloads

Shadow Data Discovery

ENTERPRISE ONLY

Scan 6 simultaneous sources – Slack, Jira, Notion, AWS S3, Google Drive, local servers – for non-compliant data hidden outside your official document store.

  1. Go to Shadow Data in the Enterprise sidebar section
  2. Review the 6 data source cards (Slack, Jira, Notion, S3, Google Drive, Servers)
  3. Click "Start Shadow Data Scan"
  4. Watch each source card animate: scanning → ✅ Clean or ⚡ ï Issues Found
  5. Review the findings table: location, data type (GDPR/HIPAA/PCI), risk level
  6. Click action buttons: Quarantine / Lock / Restrict / Review / Transfer

CCM Monitor (24/7 Alerts)

ENTERPRISE ONLY

Nightly silent cron scans every control and sends instant Slack / PagerDuty / Teams / Email alerts the moment any compliance control lapses.

  1. Go to CCM Monitor in the Enterprise sidebar section
  2. Check the Control Health Grid – green ✅ / yellow ⚡ ï / red 🔍
  3. Read the Live Alert Timeline for today's detected events
  4. Review Alert Routing: which channels get notified at each severity level
  5. Verify the Cron Schedule (0 2 * * * = daily 2AM)
  6. Click "Trigger Manual Scan Now" to run an instant check

Framework Rules Viewer

ALL PLANS

See every control rule the AI applies during scanning – control ID, description, and active status – for all 6 supported frameworks.

  1. In the sidebar, click "Framework Rules"
  2. Select a framework tab: SOC 2 / GDPR / ISO 27001 / HIPAA / PCI DSS / NIST
  3. Browse all control rules – each shows ID, name, and whether it is active
  4. Use this to prepare your documents before running a scan

🔔‹ Complete Document Scan Workflow

Here is exactly how AuditReady.AI works – from first login to receiving your audit report.

1

Sign In / Create Account

Click Log In in the top navigation bar. Sign in with Google (recommended) or your work email. New users automatically get a 14-day free trial on the Growth plan – no credit card needed.

Required: Email address & password or Google account
2

Connect Your Document Source

Go to Secure Credentials → Cloud Storage and click Connect next to your storage provider (Google Drive, OneDrive, AWS S3, Notion, Dropbox, or Box). This is a one-click OAuth flow – you grant read-only access. We never write to your files. Alternatively, skip this and simply drag-and-drop files directly into the upload zone on the Audit Scan screen.

Required: Google/Microsoft account OR your PDF/CSV/DOCX files
3

Select Compliance Framework

On the Audit Scan screen, choose which compliance standard to audit against. You can select one or more frameworks: SOC 2, GDPR, ISO 27001, HIPAA, PCI DSS, or NIST CSF. Each framework has different rule sets – the AI will apply only the relevant checks to your documents.

No credentials needed – just click the framework card
4

AI Ingests & Reads Your Documents

Click "Start AI Audit Scan". The AI engine ingests each document, chunks it into semantic segments, converts each chunk into a vector embedding, and loads it into a temporary secure memory store. This is the RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) pipeline. You will see the live terminal showing exactly which file and which rule is being checked in real time.

Fully automatic – watch the terminal scan progress bar
5

AI Detects Compliance Gaps

The AI compares each document chunk against every rule in your selected framework. If a required clause is missing (e.g. no Data Indemnity clause in a vendor contract against SOC 2 CC7.1), it is flagged as a gap with a risk score: Critical, High, Medium, or Low.

Fully automatic – results appear in the Audit Dashboard
6

Review the Audit Dashboard

The dashboard shows your Compliance Readiness Score (e.g. 71%), a table of every document with Pass/Fail status, the specific gap found, and its risk level. Failed documents show a "Fix Now" button next to them.

No action needed – review your score and gaps at a glance
7

AI Generates the Fix (Remediation)

Click "Fix Now" on any failed document. The AI generates the exact legal clause or configuration amendment needed to resolve the gap – for example, a full GDPR Article 28 processor indemnity clause, a HIPAA BAA addendum, or a corrected AWS IAM policy JSON. You can copy it to clipboard or apply it directly.

Click "Apply Fix" to mark the document as resolved in the report
8

Export the Audit Evidence Package

Once all gaps are resolved (or even before), click "Export Auditor Evidence Package". This downloads a structured report file containing your compliance score, all document results, gap descriptions, risk levels, and applied remediation amendments – ready to hand to your external auditor or regulator.

Optional: Set up email/Slack alerts to auto-send the report to your team
⟳
